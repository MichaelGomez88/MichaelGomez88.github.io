My Little Essay to You (Worth 50%)

You're so soft and warm. I mean that in a bunch of ways. I love the hugs you give and the way you hold on tightly. It's the same relief you experience after relaxing at the end of a long day. Must be the magical touch. Are the insides of your arms laced with something???? I love the way your bangs sit, your smile, your soft and rosy cheeks, the sparkle in your eyes, your beautiful eyelashes, your laugh, your nerous laughter, and so much more. I wish I could help you in more ways than I can. The cat scratches, the bruises, the irritable moods, the frustration, the overwhelming weight of these daily problems. Anything at all. I hope that when we come together you feel relieved. You're somebody who is devout in their morals and I respect that. It seems like you know what you want and what you don't want, despite how much you say "I don't know". I think you do, you just don't always want to say. You opened up so early to me, it felt as if there was a connection so early on. You know it's nuts when we were pondered and questioned about the timing of things constantly. Even if it was early, I think it was only right to say what we felt and express that care how we wanted to. I have no regrets and I hope you don't either. I enjoy the moments we spend together. I don't care if it's at a restaurant, in your car, in mine, at the arcade, out in the cold, in the wind, anywhere. I will be there by your side. There's something comforting about staring into each other's eyes and finding peace and comfort. I hope you know that I care for you deeply, Janine. There would be no other reason for me to input this effort and care into everything I do if I didn't. I like what we're working on and I like what we have. I want to expand more upon it. I know this isn't the most sophisticated or profound piece of media or work out there, but I hope you like it regardless. I had this idea and I wanted to make it real for you. It's not the grandest thing but I really do hope you like it. I can't wait for all the future things we share with each other!
